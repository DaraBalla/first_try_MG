The Siberian Tiger

The Siberian tiger is also called Amur tiger. This animal belongs to a tiger population inhabiting mainly the Primorye Province in the Russian Far East.

  • Weight: 318 kg
  • Color: rusty
  • Class: Mammalia
  • Family: Felidae

The tiger has rusty color with black transverse stripes. The body length is at least 150 cm and its big paw is adjusted to snow plains, the tiger can move on snow like with snowshoes.
